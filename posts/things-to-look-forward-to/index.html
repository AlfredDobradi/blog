Things to Look Forward To

2018-09-10 17:55
Tags: misc, music, tv

Let me tell you, September is not going well for me. I’m having some - mostly emotional - issues thanks to either myself or some external circumstances. It’s nothing huge really I’m just tired of not being able to do what I want as things go sideways when I’m thinking I’m on track. Anyway, there’s quite a few things that are either happening now or going to happen - well, or at least I hope so. Starting with the ones that are already going on.

Mayans MC

I’ve been looking forward to this for a looooong time - to be specific, since it was announced. I don’t wanna say anything about it yet as I’ve only seen the first episode and couldn’t really focus, but I like the fact it’s very different from SoA. Will re-watch once more and might write a longer thingie about it.

The Purge

I was browsing the Prime inventory the other day and I was surprised to see they adapted The Purge to TV. I dig the movies so it was a no-brainer to check the first episode out and I’m glad I did. It was decent, we didn’t get to know too much, it’s just the preliminary intro-episode I guess so we’ll see. Hope it won’t get the slash anyway.

Jack Ryan

I never read any of Tom Clancy’s books and the only reason I know his name is it was featured in copious amounts of game titles so yeah… The main reason I started watching this was John Krasinski but the premise was also pretty interesting when I first saw the trailer. I enjoy watching it, the action scenes are not bad and it seems like it tries to show the other side of the coin to some extent (I’m assuming so but obviously I’m probably not qualified to judge that aspect.).
