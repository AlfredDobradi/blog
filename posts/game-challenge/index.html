Game Challenge(?)

2018-09-11 12:23
Tags: news, game, development

The last few weeks I’ve been toying with the idea of making a game quite a lot. From time to time I have this thing but I usually don’t act on it. However… this time I started thinking about the concept I have in mind and I feel like it’s something I could do and it would be cool as heck.

Without saying too much about the game and the concept (although some people I’ve already told) it’s relatively simple graphics-wise as it would only use plain GUI elements (text boxes, frames, charts, etc) like an accounting program. I decided to go this way for two reasons: The first, more important one is it just fits the game’s premise very well. The second - which is more like a side-effect than a reason - is it’s MUCH simpler.

Having said all that, here comes the challenge part: I wanna finish the game as much as possible in 24 hours. Now I’m not sure how I will do this as I’m fairly sure coding for 24 hours is absolutely detrimental to health, so it might be sliced to two or three sessions. I intend to stream the process, but no hard promises here, for now I’m just weighing the possibilities but I feel very strongly about this and it’s been almost 16 hours since the inception of the idea so it’s not an impulse anymore.

Stay tuned, I’ll probably put something here when I make my mind up. I also have to flesh out some details about the concept itself.
